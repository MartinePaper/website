The Process

From initial contact to final sign off (not including printing) for a customized design please allow about 5-6 weeks.

Contact Me

To start the process either give me a call or fill out the form on the contact page to tell me about you and your order. I will contact you within 1-2 business days.

If you have other questions, fill out the form as best you can, and we can go from there!

Getting to know you

Once I’ve received your request, I will contact you to discuss your order. Getting to know you and providing you with one-on-one attention is a priority. Knowing as much as I can about your ideas for your special occasion enables me to provide you with the best design solution. This is also one of the best parts of my job! I absolutely love hearing all about your wedding and your ideas.

For a customized design, we will need to either meet in person (preferable), or talk over the phone (either is free). Details like the location, the color scheme, the flowers, etc. are important for me to know. We’ll also talk about your personal style, the overall vibe of the occasion and go over any images or other supplemental material you may have that illustrates your vision.

Deposit

Once you are ready to move forward, a $250 non-refundable deposit is needed to begin the design process. the deposit is applied towards the final cost of the invitation suite.

The Design

I will provide you with 2 design ideas within 3 weeks of our initial consultation. You will have three rounds of revisions before we finalize a design.

Final Proof and Approval

I will send you a final proof via PDF for you to review and sign off. At that time I will collect payment. (check and/or paypal payment) Illinois sales tax will be applied to all orders within illinois. The $250 deposit paid at the beginning of the process will be applied towards the total.

Before you sign off your approval of the final design, please proofread carefully!! This is a very important step. Have as many eyeballs as possible read the final proof, print it out on your home printer, and make sure you have included all the correct information. Double check all names, dates, titles, addresses, phone numbers, etc.

I will also check, double check and check again, all the details of each design to the best of my ability. That being said, even though we both will do our best, once in awhile, a small error can go unnoticed. If this is the case, we can re-print if necessary (if time allows, and additional printing charges apply).

Printing

Final invitations can take between 4-7 weeks to be printed and delivered depending on the complexity of the order. Due to the handcrafted nature of letterpress printing, the lead time is considerably more than with flat printing. For flat printing allow 3-4 weeks for production. Rush orders can sometimes be accommodated.

Keep in mind that the recommended mailing date for invitations is 8 to 10 weeks prior to the event date; for save the dates 6 to 8 months before the wedding date.

Shipping and Delivery

Invitations are shipped via USPS priority mail with insurance and tracking number. If you live in the chicago area I can arrange for pick-up or even personal delivery.
